Grilled Cheese

Photo taken by Ahmed Rabea. Used under the Pexels license.

Description

A dish consisting of crispy bread and melted cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp real mayonnaise
  • 2 slices crusty Italian bread
  • 2 (1 ounce) slices white Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Spread one slice of bread with real mayonnaise. Place bread Mayonnaise-side-down into hot skillet; add cheese.
  2. Spread other slice of bread with Mayonnaise and place Mayonnaise-side-up on top of cheese.
  3. Cook until lightly browned on one side. Flip over and cook until cheese is melted.
